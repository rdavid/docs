== Overview

A major part of Telco partner certification is
{url-cnf-cert}[CNF Certification Test].
These test cases verify that the Telco partner's solution, deployed on Red Hat
OpenShift, follows the {url-best-cnf}[CNF Best Practices] and the
{url-best-operator}[Operator Best Practices].
The tests confirm that the implementation meets the recommended standards for
performance and compatibility.

The suite holds {url-catalog}[88 test cases], both mandatory and optional,
split across nine suites.

== Operators

We ran the {url-cnf-cert}[CNF Certification Test] on over two hundred
{url-operators}[Red Hat operators] on OCP {ocp-version} and found an average of
thirty failures per operator.
For details, see the {url-report}[CNF Report for Operators - 20231120], where
the operator-name filter narrows the search.

== Failures

The {url-report}[CNF Report for Operators - 20231120] groups the failures by

== Known Issues

A known bug in OCP 4.12, within RHCOS 8.6, causes
{url-tainted}[`platform-alteration-tainted-node-kernel`] failures on one or
more master nodes.
The {url-tainted-tp}[Tainted Kernel Tech Preview] covers the details.
OCP 4.13 carries another {url-tainted-nft}[known bug].
We should distinguish taints that originate on our side (ideally none) from
those the CNF under test introduces.
